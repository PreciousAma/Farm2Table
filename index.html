About

According to the UN, the world is not on track to achieve Zero Hunger by 2030. 840 million will be affected by world hunger - a rise from the current 690 million. To help relieve this peril we would need to increase agricultural productivity and sustainable food production. It is undeniable that Africa has one of the world's largest agricultural marketplaces, which is why it is critical to have a service that can connect farmers all over the country. This would enable them to maximize their income by selling their farm produce at a higher price locally or where the farmer receives the highest price. For the time being, the service is free.
